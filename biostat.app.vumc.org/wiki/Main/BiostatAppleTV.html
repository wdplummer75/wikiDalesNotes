Steps to use Apple TV (for iPhone & iPad):

  • Start up the projector by pressing the HDMI button on the control panel.
  • Make sure the iPhone or iPad is connected to a Vanderbilt Wi-Fi network.
  • Turn on Bluetooth on the phone or tablet (this is so the device can discover the Apple TV).
  • On the phone or tablet, swipe up from bottom of screen to open control center.
  • Tap "AirPlay".
  • In the AirPlay balloon, tap "biostat classroom" to select. Then turn on mirroring.
  • A 4 digit code will appear on the screen. Enter that code in the device's AirPlay Code box and tap OK.

Turn off mirroring to allow another device to connect.
