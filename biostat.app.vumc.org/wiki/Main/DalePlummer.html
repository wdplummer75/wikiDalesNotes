Unless you are expecting to receive it, please do not click on a link or open an attachment in an email message even if it looks legitimate and even if you know the sender. Everything in an email message can be forged. The sender, the institution, and the result of the click can all be faked. Even if you don't give any private information, your computer could be infected with malware simply by visiting a bad site.

If you must visit a site that is mentioned in an email, type the web address into your browser or use copy and paste. Do not ever click on any links in emails. No exceptions. The convenience is not worth the risks.
